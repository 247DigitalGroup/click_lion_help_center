Account Setting

Change your password

To change your password, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your ClickLion account.

  2. Hover over your avatar to reveal the dropdown menu and click Edit Your Profile.

  3. Choose from the left menu.

  4. Fill in the form to change your password.

Recover your password

To recover your password, please follow these steps:

  1. Click here to go to the recovery form.

  2. Fill in your account email and submit the form.

  3. An email with instructions to recover your password will be sent to you within couple minutes. It might take a little longer in some unusual cases, but should not take more than 1 hour.

  4. Follow the instructions in the email to recover your password.

Update contact information

To update your contact information, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your ClickLion account.

  2. Hover over your avatar to reveal the dropdown menu and click Edit Your Profile.

  3. Fill in the form and submit the changes.

Change payment information

Payment information is very important please take all precautions to make sure you have entered all correct information. To change your payment information, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your ClickLion account.

  2. Hover over your avatar to reveal the dropdown menu and click Edit Your Profile.

  3. Choose from left menu.

  4. Fill in the form to change your payment information. The form contains following fields:

    • Payment Options: This field specifies how ClickLion sends you payments. There are two options: Pay Direct and Transfer to Wallet. If you choose Pay Direct, ClickLion will issue payments via Paypal. Bank Transfer will be available to publisher who earn more than $10,000 a month. With Transfer to Wallet option ClickLion will send funds to your wallet, in which you can then use to pay for advertisement or withdraw anytime you chooses.

    • Paypal Email: Your Paypal Account for ClickLion payments.

    • Bank Account: Necessary information in order for us to wire payments to you.

  5. Submit the form to save your information.

Your information is secured, and we will not disclose any payment information to third parties.

Your Wallet

Add funds to your wallet

To purchase a campaign, you need to have funds in your wallet. To add funds to your wallet, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your ClickLion account.

  2. Hover over your avatar to reveal the dropdown menu and click Edit Your Profile.

  3. Choose from the left menu.

  4. Fill in the Add Money to Your Balance form.

  5. Click Pay with Card to proceed.

Every time you successfully add funds to your wallet, a transaction record will be created to represent your action. Learn more on transaction types.

Withdraw funds from your wallet

To withdraw funds from your wallet, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your ClickLion account.

  2. Hover over your avatar to reveal the dropdown menu and click Edit Your Profile.

  3. Choose from the left menu.

  4. Fill in the Withdraw form.

  5. Click Withdraw to proceed.

Your minimum withdrawal amount is $100. In case the amount is smaller than $10,000, your withdrawn money will be transfered to your Paypal, otherwise it will be wire-transfered directly to your bank account.

Every time you successfully withdraw funds from your wallet, a transaction record classified as payout will be created to represent your action. Learn more on transaction types.

Your Transaction List

To access your transaction, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your ClickLion account.

  2. Hover over your avatar to reveal the dropdown menu and click Edit Your Profile.

  3. Choose from the left menu.

You should see a table of transaction which contains following columns:

  • Date: The date that the transaction is created.

  • Description: Description of the transaction.

  • Type: Type of the transaction. There are many types of transaction in ClickLion, learn more.

  • Status: Current status of the transaction. Learn more about transaction status.

  • Amount: The amount of money for that transaction.

Transaction table is read-only. History can't be rewriten!

What does ClickLion do with my personal information?

  • ClickLion uses user names in its interface and email.

  • ClickLion uses emails and encrypted passwords to authorize users.

  • ClickLion uses Paypal emails to issue payments and process withdrawals.

  • ClickLion uses bank account information (name, country, bank name, swift code, account number) to make payments and withdrawals.

  • ClickLion uses website information (mark up, stylesheet, image and content) to prepare recommendations.
