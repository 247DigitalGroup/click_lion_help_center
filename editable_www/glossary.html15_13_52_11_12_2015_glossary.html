                                          Glossary

                                          Not sure about some definitions? Learn more here
                                          • Account Management

                                          Advertiser

                                          Advertiser is used to refer to a person who is interested in bringing interested visitors to his/her site and/or refer to a top navigation section which contains advertiser related features.

                                          Campaign

                                          Campaign is used to refer to an entity that advertisers use to plan his article promotion. It is also used to refer to ClickLion report source entity. One advertiser would manage one or more campaigns at the same time.

                                          Article

                                          Article is equivalent to other Content Syndication Networks' content. It presents a content (an article, a blog post, news, announcement, ... as long as it has a title and provide useful information) that advertiser chose to promote. An Article must contain an Article Title and can contain an Article Image, Article Body and Article Publish Date. It is also used as a ClickLion report source entity. Learn more on them.

                                          Article Image

                                          Article Image is an image that best describes the meaning of the Article. Article images are used in recommendation widget (See our recommendation format). Article Image is automatically collected by Lion Collector (Learn more about how ClickLion collects article from your sites) or manually uploaded by advertisers. The image quality should be as high as possible and meets following requirements in order to gain readers attention:

                                          • Image width must be greater than or equal to 320

                                          • Image height must be greater than or equal to 240

                                          • Image ratio acceptable range (width / height): (320/240 * 0.5) < ratio < (320/240 * 2)

                                          • Allowed extensions: jpg, png

                                          Article Title

                                          Article Title is the title of your promoted article. It will be used to display on publishers' websites. Article Title should have maximum of 85 characters for best display. If it's longer than 85 characters, there's a chance that part of the title is hidden behind other Articles in the same recommendation widget.

                                          Learn more on how make your Article Title most attractive to readers.

                                          Article Body

                                          Article Body is some text that used to describe content of the Article. It is used by Lion Matcher to match with users that are interested in the content. Article Body is automatically collected by Lion Collector (Learn more about how ClickLion collects article from your sites) or manually uploaded by advertisers.

                                          Article Publish Date

                                          Article Publish Date is the date that the article is published. We used this information to not recommend readers out-dated content which doesn't have any informative value to them. It is automatically collected by Lion Collector (Learn more about how ClickLion collects article from your sites) or manually uploaded by advertisers.

                                          Publisher

                                          Publisher is used to refer to a person who wants to implement ClickLion recommendation code to his/her website to monetize his/her traffic and/or refer to a top navigation section which contains publisher related features.

                                          Domain

                                          Domain is used to present a website that publishers want to implement ClickLion recommendation code onto. It is also used to refer to ClickLion report source entity. One Publisher would manage one or more domains at the same time.

                                          Creative

                                          Creative is ClickLion recommendation widget that publishers use to implement onto their websites. Publishers can customize their creatives to display the recommendation in the way they want in order to get highest readers attention. Creative is also used as ClickLion report resource entity. One Domain can contain one or more creatives.

                                          Reader

                                          Reader is used to refer to individual person who goes to publishers' website and he/she is recommended by ClickLion.

                                          Transaction

                                          Transaction is an action record created automatically by ClickLion or manually by users (advertisers or publishers) which involves money transferring from a specific source to specific destination.

                                          Report

                                          Report refers to report section on the top navigation bar. It also refers to the performance report for a source entity (a campaign, an article, a domain or a creative) in a specific date range with different frequencies (hourly, daily or monthly). A report contains many statistics on many metrics and intuitive graphs for advertisers and publishers to track their performance conveniently.

                                          Impression

                                          Impression is a report metric used by ClickLion report system. It is counted whenever an individual creative on publisher websites is loaded. If a webpage of a publisher is implemented with 2 creatives, there are 2 impressions counted for that publishers domain if that webpage is loaded by a reader.

                                          Page View

                                          Page View is a report metric used by ClickLion report system. It is counted whenever a webpage of a publisher is visited and loaded by a reader.

                                          Page View is always equal to or lower than Impression for a report source entity. If the source entity is a creative, they are equal. If the source entity is a domain, Page View is likely to be lower thanImpression because when a web page is loaded, Page View is counted as 1 but Impression might be counter as 2 or 3 based on number of creatives implemented on that page.

                                          Click

                                          Click is a report metric used by ClickLion report system. Whenever a reader clicks a recommended article, Click will be counted as 1 for the clicked article, its campaign, the creative displaying that article and the domain of that creative.

                                          CTR (Click Through Rate)

                                          CTR is the most important metric to measure how effective our recommendation is. CTR of a report entity is the number of clicks divided by the number of page view for that entity.

                                          CTR = Clicks / Page View

                                          For example, if your creative received 5 clicks out of 1000 page views, your creative CTR would be .5%.

                                          When the report entity does not have any page view, CTR will be displayed as 0.

                                          Estimated Earning

                                          Estimated Earning is a report metric used by ClickLion report system. It is only available for a few report source entities (domain, creative). For other source entities such as campaigns and articles, see Estimated Spending.

                                          Estimated Earning is an estimation of the revenue you are getting from your domains. Because it is a rough estimation, Estimated Earning might be calibrated later according to our Policies.

                                          Estimated Spending

                                          Estimated Spending is one of ClickLion report metric. It is only available for some report source entity (campaign, article). For other source entities such as domain and creative, see Estimated Earning.

                                          Estimated Spending is estimation of the budget you spent for the campaign. Being an estimation, Estimated Spending might be calibrated later due to our Policies.

                                          CPC (Cost per Click)

                                          CPC is a report metric used by ClickLion report system. It is the average cost of a click and is calculated as:

                                          CPC = Estimated Earning / Clicks

                                          For example, if a domain receives 100 clicks and its estimated earning is $1.5, CPC is $0.015 (15 cents).

                                          If a source entity does not have any clicks, CPC will be displayed as 0.

                                          Displayed Rate

                                          Display Rate is a report metric used by ClickLion report system. It is only available for these report source entities: creatives, articles.

                                          Display Rate of a report source entity is the percentage of number of pageviews that the recommendation is actually displayed out of all pageviews. It may be slightly smaller than 100% because sometime ClickLion is blocked by AdBlock or web browsers are not compatible (Javascript is disabled or ClickLion script is illegally injected).
